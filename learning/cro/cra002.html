If you are working at a site and are new to the clinical trial quality concept, then this bundle of three site quality related courses will provide you with the basic knowledge and skills to help you set up your own quality systems and procedures.

This package includes the following ACRP eLearning courses at a 20% discount:

  • Site Quality Management Tools: SOPs, Metrics, and Training
  • Key Skills for Ensuring Quality Control through Risk-Based Decision Making
  • Building Quality Management Systems for Sites and Sponsors: Root Cause and CAPA

Important Note: Please begin with the site quality management tools course and then take the risk-based decision making course.
